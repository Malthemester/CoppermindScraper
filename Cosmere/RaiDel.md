**RaiDel** is a pepper grown in [[JinDo\|JinDo]] on [[Sel\|Sel]]. It is a key ingredient of [[RaiDomo Mai\|RaiDomo Mai]].
In the year prior to the [[Invasion of Arelon\|Invasion of Arelon]], RaiDel peppers had a poor season.

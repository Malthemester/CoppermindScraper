The **Sea of Lennes** is a body of water adjacent to the [[Elendel Basin\|Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. It lies south of the [[Sea of Yomend\|Sea of Yomend]] and makes up the southwest coast of the Basin.

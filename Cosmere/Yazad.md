**Yazad** was the forty-eighth Emperor of the [[Rose Empire\|Rose Empire]] on [[Sel\|Sel]].
Near the end of his reign, he grew weak with sickness. After his death, [[Ashravan\|Ashravan]] succeeded him as the emperor.

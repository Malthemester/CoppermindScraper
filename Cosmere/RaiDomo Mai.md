**RaiDomo Mai** is a [[JinDo\|JinDo]] dish on [[Sel\|Sel]], whose name means "meat with fiery skin."
It is made with meat and vegetables, including [[RaiDel\|RaiDel]] peppers, boiled in a red sauce.
